Welcome to the Classic Game Shuffle


What is a game shuffle you ask? I have selected five random games from my collection and we will be playing them. Heres how this works, we will start with one of the games chosen at random. This game will be played for an unknown period of time and then without warning, we will be shuffled to another game. That game will be played for an unknown period of time then shuffled again, and so on. Shuffles can happen at any time. Maybe it will happen when you were about to parry that bullet or mid jump over a pit. Better be ready when you return.
